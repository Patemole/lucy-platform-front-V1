Academics

At Penn, students are exposed to interdisciplinary experiences and knowledge, enhancing their academic experience across our four schools. By offering an inclusive, hands-on education rooted in liberal arts and sciences and driven by research, our students leave campus ready to apply their learnings and make a difference worldwide.
