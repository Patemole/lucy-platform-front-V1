“Bookkeeper” role

You can now grant your bookkeepers access to your Tola account with the new “Bookkeeper” role, enabling them to see all your invoices and bills on Tola.

Users can invite your bookkeeper by navigating to Settings > Members, ensuring your bookkeeper can view all bills and invoices for reconciliation and overview purposes.
