Outcomes

If you’re looking to become a part of a community of self-starters, innovators, and leaders, you’ve come to the right place. Our Quakers span the globe, yet they all share a common trait— our graduates emerge from our schools and programs ready to make a meaningful impact on the world around them. 

 Whether they decide to enter their careers right away, or further their studies first, our students utilize the skills, knowledge, and experiences they've acquired during their time at Penn to forge their own paths to success. 

And if you don’t believe us (we do encourage inquisitive thinking after all), let our statistics* speak for themselves.
