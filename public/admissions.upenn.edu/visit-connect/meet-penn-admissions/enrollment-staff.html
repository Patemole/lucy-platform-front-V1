Enrollment Staff

Meet our staff! Admissions Officers (AOs) are here to help answer your questions about all things Penn, from applying, to academics and campus life.

At Penn, admissions officers are responsible for reading applications from different regions. We divide the United States into West and Midwest, Northeast, Mid-Atlantic, and South. We also have a team dedicated to reading our International applications from around the globe! When looking to contact an admissions officer make sure they are the appropriate contact for your region. We look forward to connecting with you!

Don't see your state or country listed? Reach out to us at info@admissions.upenn.edu.

Mid-Atlantic Region

Our Mid-Atlantic Team reads applications from Delaware, Maryland, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, and Washington, D.C..

Contact our Mid-Atlantic team at midatlanticteam@admissions.upenn.edu

Northeast Region

Our Northeast Team reads applications from Connecticut, Maine, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, New York, Rhode Island, and Vermont.

Contact our Northeast team at northeastteam@admissions.upenn.edu

South Region

Our South team reads applications from Alabama, Arkansas, Florida, Georgia, Louisiana, Kentucky, Mississippi, North Carolina, Oklahoma, Puerto Rico, South Carolina, Tennessee, Texas, U.S. Virgin Islands, Virginia, and West Virginia.

Contact our South team at southteam@admissions.upenn.edu

West/Midwest

Our West/Midwest team reads applications from Alaska, Arizona, California, Colorado, Guam, Hawaii, Idaho, Illinois, Indiana, Iowa, Kansas, Michigan, Minnesota, Missouri, Montana, Nebraska, Nevada, New Mexico, North Dakota, Ohio, Oregon, South Dakota, Utah, Washington, Wisconsin, and Wyoming.

Contact our West/Midwest team at westmidwestteam@admissions.upenn.edu

International Region

Our International team reads all applications from outside the U.S.—they're experts at getting to know students from all over the world!

Contact our International team at internationalteam@admissions.upenn.edu
