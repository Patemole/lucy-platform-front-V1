First-Generation Applicants

Will you be the first in your family to go to college? Congratulations! We know this is an exciting- and even daunting – time. We’re here to support you! Nearly 1/5 of the class of 2027 were in the same boat as you, so believe us when we say you can do it – and you won’t be alone!
