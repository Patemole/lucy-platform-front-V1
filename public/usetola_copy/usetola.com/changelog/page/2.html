Changelog

March 1, 2024

Micro-deposit bank verification

You can link any US bank account to Tola with the new micro-deposit bank verification, enabling you to pay and get paid via Tola.

To access this navigate to Settings > Bank Accounts > Add Bank Account > Verify with Manual Deposit, allowing you to connect any US bank accounts your business might have.

Learn more in our FAQ.

February 17, 2024

Subscription bill attachments

You can access detailed Tola billing information with the new subscription bill attachments.

To see this information, select a bill from Tola, download the .pdf attachment, and view what Tola plan you were on.

December 30, 2023

QuickBooks live-sync integration

Forget manual reconciliation and view live accounting data in Tola—our new QuickBooks live-sync integration enables a seamless live sync between Tola and QuickBooks.

You can enable this feature in Settings > Accounting, ensuring that all bills paid on Tola sync correctly with QuickBooks and allowing you to pay and collect all your bills and invoices from Quickbooks via Tola.

Learn more in our FAQ.

December 13, 2023

Intuitive bill payments calendar

You can make paying a bill more intuitive and straightforward with the new bill payments calendar, allowing you to easily see and choose a payment date and how it affects the receiving date.

This feature will be available every time you go to pay bills, providing a clear indication and easy choice of payment dates during the bill payment process.

December 10, 2023

Referrals

Earn cash rewards by referring Tola to your network.

You can find this feature in Settings > Refer a Company, allowing you to benefit while helping others discover the advantages of Tola.

Learn more in our FAQ.

December 5, 2023

Automated invoice reminders

Streamline your revenue collection with our new automated invoice reminders feature, which sends notifications about upcoming and overdue invoices directly to your customers.

You can access and enable this feature in Settings > Reminders or during the invoice creation flow, ensuring you spend less time chasing upcoming and overdue invoices.

Learn more in our FAQ.

December 4, 2023

Eliminate duplicates

Payments for duplicate bills are prevented with the new eliminate duplicates improvement, saving you from the error of paying the same bill twice.

We now detect and flag duplicate bills, helping you avoid the human error of adding and paying duplicate bills.

Learn more in our FAQ.

December 1, 2023

Tiers

Know your payment times and limits easily with the new Tiers feature, which allows users to see information about payment speed on Tola and their overall usage.

You can access this feature in Settings > Tiers, providing valuable insights into your activity on Tola and your payment capabilities.

Learn more in our FAQ.

September 25, 2023

Saving payment details

We're making invoice payments hassle-free by allowing your customers to save their payment details for future payments.

Your customers will be able to save their payment details by accessing the payment link on an invoice, enabling them to make faster payments the next time they pay on Tola.

Learn more in our FAQ.

September 23, 2023

Resolving payment issues

You can quickly resolve failed payments for bills with the new resolving payment issues improvement.

You will receive an email notification and see a Payment Failed status in Tola on bills that experience payment failure, allowing you to address the issue promptly and ensure timely payments to vendors.

Learn more in our FAQ.
